I’ve always been obsessed with people’s hand writing. Reading their writings is like deciphering their thoughts and sentiments. After learning about how Toshi Omagari made Sir Quentin Blake typeface, which is described “…doesn’t just look like his writing, it acts like it too ”, it immediately struck me by the gerund of my friend Hehe’s journal as it gave me a sense of tender & vigour.

I spent my last three days making this typeface, and here it is! Huge thanks to Hehe and all friendly and gifted individuals at type school :)

– Yolly 2018.07.26 @Tongji
